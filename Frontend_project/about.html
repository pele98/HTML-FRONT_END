Introduction

Fake band - founded in early 2015, is a fake band for a website project. We are a group of 5 friends, interested in music and we do varieties of different music, our own as well as covers.

Guitarist

Our guitarist, Kermit the frog, has been with the band since the beginning. He has been playing guitar all his life and is loved for his jumping of roofs stunts on stage.

Basists

Harambe has been our bass player since 2017, after his retirement from a local zoo. She also helps the bandmates with the stage arrangements and she loves going around the crowd and holding our fans childs.

Drummer

Our drummer, Elmo, is a valuable resource for the band. Besides his drumming skills, he can keep an eye on the crowd, due to his super sized eyes. He also has a unique drumset, which includes all normal drum equipment as well as some thrash cans.

Singer

Little My was our obvious choice for the singes. She capsulates everyone with her angelic voice, and is totally tall enough to reach the microphone.

5th instrument and back-up singer

The bands secret weapon is our multitalented back-up singer, Peppa the pig. She can play any instrument that we need and is big (7'1) and strong enough to also work as our security officer.
